# Frequently Asked Questions

---

## Is AnyKeyboard free?

Yes. The core functionality is free to use.

---

## Do I need to install anything?

No. AnyKeyboard runs directly in your browser.

---

## Does it work on mobile?

Yes. It works on both desktop and mobile devices.

---

## Is my typing saved?

No. Typed text is not stored or logged.

---

## Is this a translator?

No. AnyKeyboard helps you type — it does not translate text.

---

## What is phonetic typing?

Phonetic typing lets you type using Latin letters (based on sound) and outputs
the target script. It’s available for major languages where supported.

---

## What is the speed writing utility?

It’s a set of writing helpers built into AnyKeyboard to reduce friction and help
you type faster across all supported languages and layouts.

---

## Can I use it offline?

Currently, an internet connection is required.

---

## Is this safe to use?

Yes. All typing happens client-side in your browser.

Try it here:
https://anykeyboard.io
